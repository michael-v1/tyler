BASTARD

Tyler, The Creator

December 25, 2009

Tyler, the Creator's debut album "Bastard" (2009) introduced his provocative style, blending gritty beats with introspective lyrics. It explores themes of personal struggle, identity, and societal alienation, establishing Tyler as a bold voice in hip-hop. The album's controversial content and raw honesty garnered both acclaim and criticism, solidifying Tyler's reputation as an artist unafraid to push boundaries.
